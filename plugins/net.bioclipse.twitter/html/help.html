Twitter in Bioclipse

The twitter feature in Bioclipse let you write tweets from within Bioclipse. After installing the feature: Open the Bioclipse Preferences (Window > Preferences) and then choose Twitter Settings in the list to the left. Fill in your Twitter username and password and press b>OK.

Once you have registered your Twitter account in Bioclipse, then just go to the JavaScript console. If it isn't opened the you find it in Window > Show View > Others... and then in the menu that is opened: Scripting > JavaScript console. Now to send a tweet from Bioclipse all you have to do is to use the following command in the JavaScript console:

twitter.setStatus

For example, to say hello to all your followers you can write:

twitter.setStatus("Hello, followers! I'm writing to you from within Bioclipse!")
